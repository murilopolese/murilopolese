About

As a creative technologist, consultant, researcher, and full-stack developer with experience in interactive media art for installations and advertisement campaigns, I specialize in developing robust systems, databases, embed software and infrastructure.

My current focus is on education and technology and I've worked with companies such as Strawbees, Quirkbot, Kano, Joy Labz, Makey Makey and Arduino. As an ambassador of Kids Hack Day and Science Hack Day hackathons, I'm passionate about participating in learning that celebrates creativity, agency and critical thinking.

I've facilitated courses at School for Poetic Computation, Konsftack, Berghs, Hyper Island, and Vefskoli.
